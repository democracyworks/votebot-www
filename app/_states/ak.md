## Voter ID

You will need to show ID to vote in Alaska.

Acceptable forms of ID include: official voter registration card; driver's license; birth certificate; passport; hunting or fishing license; current utility bill, bank statement, paycheck, government check or other government document with the voterâ€™s name and address.
